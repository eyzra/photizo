The Definition

NOT A NOUN.
A VERB.

Photizo (Greek: phōtizō) does not merely describe the presence of light, but the aggressive action of illumination.

At Photizo Global, we do not just inform; we Illuminate. We believe information fills the head, but illumination transforms the spirit. We refuse to traffic in theories of a God we have not encountered; we traffic in the revelation of His person.

"We have a mandate to turn men from darkness to light and from the power of Satan to the power of God."

A City Set on a Hill

Photizo Global is a didactic and systematic teaching ministry founded by Apostle Ezra Anya. Our vision is to make all men see Jesus, turning hearts from darkness to light and compelling believers into the fullness of their divine ordination.

Our services are patterned after the Apostolic model, where sound doctrine meets the supernatural. We do not just explain God; we experience Him.

Illuminating the Spirit

At Photizo, we are committed to not just informing the mind, but illuminating the spirit. We emphasize the supremacy of Jesus in doctrine, the steadfastness of Jesus in discipleship, the sufficiency of Jesus in devotion, and the superiority of Jesus in the demonstration of power.

"We stand as the light of the world... darkness is exposed, chains are broken, and men are transformed into the very image of the Son."

STATEMENT OF FAITH

What we believe defines how we live.

The Scriptures

We believe the Bible is the inspired, inerrant, and authoritative Word of God. It is the final court of arbitration for all matters of doctrine and conduct.

The Triune God

We believe in one God, eternally existing in three persons: Father, Son, and Holy Spirit—co-equal in power and glory.

The Person of Jesus

We believe in the deity of our Lord Jesus Christ, His virgin birth, His sinless life, His atoning death, His bodily resurrection, and His ascension to the right hand of the Father.

Salvation

We believe that salvation is by grace alone, through faith alone, in Christ alone. It is a free gift of God, not of works.

The Holy Spirit

We believe in the present-day ministry of the Holy Spirit, whose indwelling enables the Christian to live a godly life and whose power empowers the believer for witness and miraculous demonstration.

The Church

We believe the Church is the Body of Christ, the habitation of God through the Spirit, with a divine appointment to fulfill the Great Commission.

The Resurrection

We believe in the resurrection of both the saved and the lost; they that are saved unto the resurrection of life, and they that are lost unto the resurrection of judgment.
